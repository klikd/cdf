# CRM Status Codes

This document contains all CRM status codes with their detailed information including operational descriptions, objectives, requirements, permissions, and transition rules.

## Status Categories

- **استقبال (Reception)**: Initial receipt and registration of reports
- **تقييم (Evaluation)**: Assessment and classification of reports
- **تحقيق (Investigation)**: Investigation activities and procedures
- **انتظار (Waiting)**: Pending states requiring external input
- **قرار (Decision)**: Decision-making and approval processes
- **تنفيذ (Execution)**: Implementation of decisions and actions
- **استثناء (Exception)**: Cases closed due to various exceptions
- **إغلاق (Closure)**: Final closure states
- **أرشفة (Archival)**: Archival and retention states
- **تظلّم (Appeal)**: Appeal and review processes
- **متابعة لاحقة (Post-Follow-up)**: Follow-up activities after closure
- **تحويل (Transfer)**: Transfer and referral states
- **معالجة (Processing)**: General processing states
- **رقابة (Control)**: Quality control and review states
- **حماية (Protection)**: Protection and security states
- **تعليق (Suspension)**: Suspension states
- **تصعيد (Escalation)**: Escalation states
- **مراجعة (Review)**: Review and assessment states
- **إجراء (Action)**: Specific action states
